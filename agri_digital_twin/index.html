About AgriTwin

Transforming agriculture with AI and Digital Twin technology

Our Mission

AgriTwin leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence, satellite imagery, and digital twin technology to empower farmers with data-driven insights. Our platform helps predict crop health, optimize yields, and make informed decisions about what to grow and when to harvest.

🚀 Technologies Used

🛰️

Satellite Imaging

ISRO BHUVAN (OPIE)

High-resolution multispectral satellite imagery from India's space program provides real-time monitoring of crop health, vegetation indices, and land use patterns.

  • 10m spatial resolution
  • NDVI & NDWI analysis
  • Weekly image updates
  • Historical data archive
🤖

Machine Learning

Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch

Advanced ML models trained on millions of data points to predict crop yields, disease risks, and optimal harvest timing with high accuracy.

  • Deep learning for image analysis
  • Time-series forecasting
  • Anomaly detection
  • Continuous model improvement
✨

Generative AI

GPT-4, LLaMA, Custom Models

Natural language processing and generative AI provide personalized recommendations, answer farmer queries, and generate actionable insights from complex data.

  • Conversational AI assistant
  • Automated report generation
  • Multi-language support
  • Context-aware suggestions
🌐

Digital Twin

IoT + AI + Simulation

Create virtual replicas of farms that mirror real-world conditions and allow simulation of different scenarios before making critical farming decisions.

  • Real-time synchronization
  • What-if scenario analysis
  • Physics-based modeling
  • Predictive optimization
